# MASTIC

Modular, Adaptive, System-Tailored Inference Connector.

MASTIC is being designed as one guided CLI and TUI for configuring and
operating host-tailored inference stacks. Its controller, gateway, lifecycle,
and adapter boundaries are being resolved through the
[Phase 1 Wayfinder map](https://github.com/nisavid/mastic/issues/1) before
product implementation begins.

The current mlxctl and codex-ns-proxy implementations are preserved with their
histories and behavior under `legacy/`. Research informing the product and
architecture decisions lives under `docs/research/`.
